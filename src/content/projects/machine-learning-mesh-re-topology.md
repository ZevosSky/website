This project is an attempt to learn machine learning by building around a problem space I care about: clean geometry, artist workflows, and rig-ready meshes.

The current pass focuses on understanding the pipeline end to end. That means dataset prep, simple experiments, and learning how libraries such as PyTorch, Trimesh, and graph-based tooling fit together.

## Why this matters

I like projects that force multiple disciplines to overlap. This one sits in the middle of graphics engineering, technical art, and ML experimentation, which makes it a good long-term anchor for the portfolio. 

## Thesis

If I can train a moddel to fix moddels that are of poor topology I can accelerate artist work flows and even possibly make AI generated meshes viable in projects. Wether it comes from zbrush/blender or an AI moddel there are a few things they all share in common: Inconsisstent tri size & poor topology for animation via a lack of edge loops.
